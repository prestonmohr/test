Mission Statement

At Meridian Holdings Group, we believe in turning the cash value in life insurance policies into a safe, stable investment opportunity. Our mission is to provide institutional investors with a secure, liquid, and premium investment vehicle backed by the cash values of life insurance policies. By combining world-class asset backing, strong liquidity, and a smart structure, we deliver peace of mind and consistent returns.

What We Do

We specialize in offering senior secured notes backed by the cash values of life insurance policies. Investors are further protected by pledged company equity. Our senior notes are expected to carry a high investment-grade rating from at least major NRSRO. Our structure ensures:

Collateralized Assets

Policies from highly rated carriers with typically 150+ years in business and strong regulatory oversight.

Layered Protection

First lien security plus subordinated equity creating multiple layers of investor protection.

Attractive Returns

Target yields of 100 basis points above comparable maturing U.S. Treasuries.

Risk Management

Active monitoring, policy replacement capabilities, and trustee oversight through UMB Bank.

Investment Structure & Security

Our investment structure is designed with multiple layers of protection to ensure investor security while delivering premium returns. The creative architecture of our offering creates the robust framework that prioritizes safety without compromising performance.

Smart Structure

Investor notes are protected by a first lien on policy cash values and further secured by subordinated equity from Meridian Holdings Group's parent company, creating layered security and transparency.

Strong Partners

We work with United Missouri Bank (UMB) as trustee - the second-largest trustee bank in the U.S. - ensuring professional oversight and compliance.

Investor Repayment

Multiple excess cash flow strategies ensure you get paid: policy growth exceeding requirements, refinancing options, death benefits, or direct access to policy cash values.

Portfolio Management

Active risk management includes replacing paid-out policies with new collateral during the financing term, maintaining portfolio quality and value.
