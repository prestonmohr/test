Mausmi Pala

Chief Compliance Officer

Mausmi serves as the Chief Compliance Officer of Meridian Holdings Group.

Mausmi holds a master’s degree in Financial Analysis and Quantitative Risk Management, has successfully completed the CFA Level 1 exam, and is currently pursuing the CFA Level 2. With a strong foundation in corporate finance, risk management, and portfolio analysis, she brings extensive expertise to her role as Chief Compliance Officer.

In her capacity as CCO, she is responsible for overseeing regulatory compliance within the organization, ensuring strict adherence to industry standards and legal requirements. Leveraging her deep understanding of financial data and regulatory frameworks, Mausmi provides valuable insights to guide compliance strategies that foster sustainable operations and mitigate risks.

Committed to upholding the highest standards of compliance, Mausmi is dedicated to ensuring our firm operates ethically and responsibly within regulatory frameworks. Mausmi plays a pivotal role in safeguarding our firm’s reputation and maintaining the trust of our clients and stakeholders by fostering a culture of compliance and integrity.
