Kirk Stafford

Chief Executive Officer

Kirk serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Meridian Holdings Group.

Kirk began trading exchange-traded options in 1996. His expertise in derivatives and exchange options enabled the creation of the IOP strategy, which is designed to capture the full potential of the market while also creating a reduced risk profile. He first began using the strategy with his own money and quickly realized how the IOP could resonate with clients. The potential performance with limited downside risk is a true advantage in any portfolio.

In his spare time, Kirk resides with his wife in Meridian, Idaho. He adores spending time with his family, grandchildren, and “Suzy,” his favorite calculator. He is also a dedicated member of his church community, where he regularly participates in community events.

Turning cash value into opportunity through institutional-grade, short-term notes backed by high-quality life insurance policies.
